Wisteria sinensis (fuji; Noda-fuji; Chinese wisteria)

Wisteria symbolizes resilience and longevity, as this stunning plant can live for centuries. However,lavender or blue wisteria, as depicted in The Wisteria Maiden, can also symbolize rejection and lost love. Resilence and longevity. Wisterias symbolize romance in most cultures where the plants are native. In Korea in particular, the Wister flower carries the meaning of devotion that transcends death. The Japanese also consider Wisteria a symbol of good luck, success, and longevity
